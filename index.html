Game Rules

game objective:

get your total score to the final score or above,the final score value can be set by you only at the start of each game

how to play:

set the final score to whatever you want and start by rolling the dice, the number you rolled will be added to your current score,but be careful... if the dice shows '1' your current and total score are reset to 0 and its the other player's turns

what is the "hold" button?

when you want to, you can pass your turn, and possibly let the opponent get to the final score before you do. so why should you ever do that? 10% percent of the final score will be added to your total score along with your current score!

things to note:

1. you cannot hold without rolling at least once on each turn
2. you cannot hold if the score added will make you win
